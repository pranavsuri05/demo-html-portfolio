The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

ZNMD

A soul-stirring adventure that celebrates friendship, self-discovery, and the thrill of living life to the fullest.

Crazy Rich Asians

A dazzling, heartwarming rom-com that offers a peek into lavish lifestyles while exploring love, family dynamics, and cultural identity.

A vibrant blend of romance and drama that showcases the magic of love and the challenges of blending contrasting worlds.
